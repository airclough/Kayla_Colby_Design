about

I always was more into the technical aspect of art and I realized in high school that it could translate into advertising and graphic design, so that led me to minor in graphic design and major in Visual Communications and start to make wearable designs for Bad Kids Clothing. I’m a big graphics & typography person. I love clean lines mixed with a grunge element. I love working with font, editing it and playing with shapes and how they fit. I like subtly witty tag lines and making them into graphics. I’ll brainstorm with words, font, styles, and color from anything I see interesting. I’m a huge thinker. I want to explore every possible option and style from anyone and everyone.

I wake up and start looking at ways to design an idea.

If something inspires me I can go off on a completely different style of design. I’m a big fan of pop art and Andy Warhol (cliché I know), as well as David Carson who designed for Ray Gun with his unconventional Grunge text design, and Paul Rand for his logo design.
